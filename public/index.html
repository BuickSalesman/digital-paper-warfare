Getting Started: The Drawing Phase

  1. Draw Your Obstacles:
    • Create up to five obstacles on the game board. These barriers can shape the battlefield to your advantage. While you don't have to use all five, maximizing your creativity is highly recommended for the best strategic edge!

  2. Edit Your Masterpiece:
    • Not satisfied with a shape? No worries! You can erase your most recent drawing by hitting the Erase button and try again.
  3. Rules for Drawing:
    • No Nested Shapes: Shapes cannot be drawn inside or engulf other shapes.
    • No Overlapping: While your shapes can intersect with themselves, they cannot intersect with shapes drawn by your opponent .
    • Respect the No-Drawing Zones: Avoid the designated areas around the fortresses and the margins around the board to prevent complete blockades.
    • Ink Management: You have a limited amount of ink for each shape, so draw wisely to optimize your defenses and strategies!
  4. Time is Ticking:
    • The drawing phase lasts for 60 seconds or until both players have either completed their five shapes or are ready to proceed.

Enter the Battle Phase

  1. Turn-Based Combat:
    • The battle unfolds in turns , each lasting 30 seconds .
    • One Action per Turn: On your turn, choose to either move or shoot with your tanks or utilize your turret‚Äôs shooting capabilities.
    • Tanks vs. Turrets:
      • Tanks: Agile units that can move across the battlefield or take precise shots at your enemy.
      • Turrets: Defensive structures that can shoot but remain stationary, providing steady firepower.
  2. Heat Management:
    • Action Activation: Click on a tank or turret to perform an action. This starts the power thermometer , which gauges the heat level.
    • Heat Dynamics:
      • Optimal Heat: Hit the right heat level for a more powerful shot.
      • Overheating: Excessive heat can lead to misfires, so balance your actions carefully.
    • Directional Control: Move or shoot by dragging your mouse in the opposite direction of your intended action‚Äî master this mechanic to enhance your tactical play!
  3. Health and Strategy:
    • Tanks: Each tank has 2 hit points .
    • Reactors: Each reactor has 1 hit point .
    • Fortresses and Obstacles: While fortresses are indestructible, tanks cannot move through shapes or fortresses , requiring you to strategize your movements and attacks thoughtfully.
    • Shooting Through Walls: Tanks can shoot through fortress walls , allowing for strategic offensive plays even behind defenses.
  4. Time Pressure:
    • Fast-Paced Turns: Each turn is 30 seconds , demanding quick and decisive actions.
    • Ticking Clock Penalty: Fail to make a move within the allotted time, and your next turn will be only 3 seconds long until you perform a valid action again. Stay sharp and keep the pressure on!
